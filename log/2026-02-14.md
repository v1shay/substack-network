## Virtual environment and Anaconda

- **Anaconda vs venv:** When Anaconda is active, `python` can point at Conda even after `source venv/bin/activate`. We do **not** patch the generated venv `activate` script.
- **Solution:** Custom `activate.sh` at project root: deactivates conda if active, then sources `venv/bin/activate` and forces shell to use venv’s `python`. Documented as the recommended way to activate. Optional: `../venv/bin/python scripts/milestone01.py` for running without activating.
